Greetings, traveler!

Do you want to steal some panties? Yes, you do! HA-HA-HA-HA-HA

Good news, you can cast Steal. To do that, open a girl's portrait and hold left mouse button. That will cost you 1 mana.

Beware: girls might not like you stealing. Watch your health!

To watch your progress, open the diary. You can find it in your backpack.

Visit locations and search for the items. To discover them, slowly hover over the location. An item will appear when you find the right place.

Good luck!

- Secret Adventurer
